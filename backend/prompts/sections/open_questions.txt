## Section Guidelines

This is a CRITICAL section for DRAFT PRDs. Identify all questions that need answers:

**Question Categories:**

### Business Questions
- Revenue model and pricing decisions
- Market positioning and competitive strategy
- Go-to-market approach
- Resource allocation and budget

### Technical Questions
- Architecture decisions pending
- Technology choices to make
- Integration unknowns
- Scalability approach

### User/UX Questions
- User research gaps
- Usability decisions
- Accessibility requirements
- User flow uncertainties

### Scope Questions
- Feature prioritization unclear
- MVP vs. future phases
- What's in vs. out of scope

**For each question include:**
- **Question**: Clear, specific question
- **Context**: Why this matters / impact of not answering
- **Suggested Owner**: Who should answer this (PM, Engineering, Design, etc.)
- **Priority**: Critical / High / Medium

**Format:**
### [Category]
1. **[Question]**
   - Context: [Why this matters]
   - Owner: [Role/Team]
   - Priority: [Critical/High/Medium]

Be thorough - it's better to surface more questions than to miss important ones.

Length: 10-20 questions organized by category.
