## Section Guidelines

Define how success will be measured:

**For each metric include:**
- **Metric Name**: Clear, descriptive name
- **Definition**: Exactly what is being measured and how
- **Baseline**: Current state (if known) or "To be established"
- **Target**: Specific target value
- **Timeframe**: When the target should be achieved
- **Measurement Method**: How data will be collected

**Metric Categories:**
- **Business Metrics**: Revenue, cost savings, market share
- **User Metrics**: Adoption, engagement, satisfaction (NPS, CSAT)
- **Product Metrics**: Feature usage, completion rates, error rates
- **Operational Metrics**: Support tickets, deployment frequency

Format as a table:
| Metric | Baseline | Target | Timeframe | Measurement |
|--------|----------|--------|-----------|-------------|
| User adoption | 0 | 1000 MAU | 3 months | Analytics |

Length: 6-10 key metrics covering business and product outcomes.
