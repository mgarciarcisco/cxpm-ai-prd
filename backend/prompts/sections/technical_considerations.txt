## Section Guidelines

Document technical architecture and constraints:

**Areas to cover:**
- **Architecture Overview**: High-level system design or constraints
- **Technology Stack**: Recommended or required technologies (if specified)
- **Integration Points**: Systems this solution must integrate with
  - APIs to consume or expose
  - Data sources to connect to
  - Third-party services
- **Data Requirements**:
  - Data entities and relationships
  - Storage requirements
  - Data migration needs
- **Infrastructure**: Hosting, deployment, environment requirements
- **Technical Constraints**: Limitations or restrictions that impact design
- **Technical Debt**: Known technical debt to address or avoid creating

Focus on constraints and decisions that impact product development, not implementation details.

Length: Comprehensive coverage of technical context (3-5 subsections).
