## Section Guidelines

Identify and plan for project risks:

**Risk Categories:**
- **Technical Risks**: Technology challenges, integration issues, performance concerns
- **Business Risks**: Market changes, budget constraints, stakeholder alignment
- **Resource Risks**: Team availability, skill gaps, dependencies on key people
- **Timeline Risks**: Scope creep, external dependencies, estimation errors
- **User Risks**: Adoption challenges, usability issues, change resistance

**For each risk include:**
- **Risk ID**: RISK-001, RISK-002, etc.
- **Category**: Technical / Business / Resource / Timeline / User
- **Description**: What could go wrong
- **Likelihood**: High / Medium / Low
- **Impact**: High / Medium / Low
- **Mitigation Strategy**: How to prevent or reduce the risk
- **Contingency Plan**: What to do if the risk occurs
- **Owner**: Who is responsible for monitoring (if identifiable)

**Format as risk matrix:**
| ID | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Contingency |
|----|------|------------|--------|------------|-------------|
| RISK-001 | ... | Medium | High | ... | ... |

Length: 6-10 significant risks with mitigation strategies.
