## Section Guidelines

Provide a clear action plan to move from DRAFT to complete PRD:

**Structure:**

### Immediate Actions (This Week)
Actions that should happen right away:
- Schedule stakeholder meetings
- Gather missing information
- Clarify critical questions

### Short-term Actions (Next 2-4 Weeks)
Actions to address major gaps:
- Conduct user research
- Technical spike/POC
- Business case development

### Before PRD Finalization
Checklist of items needed before PRD can be marked complete:
- [ ] All open questions answered
- [ ] Stakeholder sign-off obtained
- [ ] Technical feasibility confirmed
- [ ] Resource allocation confirmed

**For each action include:**
- **Action**: Specific, actionable task
- **Owner**: Role or person responsible
- **Timeline**: When it should be done
- **Dependency**: What it depends on (if any)
- **Output**: What deliverable is expected

**Format:**
### [Timeframe]
| Action | Owner | Timeline | Output |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| ... | ... | ... | ... |
