## Section Guidelines

Create SMART goals and clear objectives:
- **Primary Goal**: The main outcome this initiative aims to achieve
- **Secondary Objectives**: Supporting objectives that contribute to the primary goal
- **Success Criteria**: Specific, measurable criteria for each goal (quantify where possible)
- **Business Alignment**: How these goals align with broader organizational objectives
- **Key Results**: Concrete deliverables or outcomes expected

Format goals with clear structure:
- Goal 1: [Description]
  - Success Criteria: [Measurable outcome]
  - Timeline: [If specified]

Length: Include 2-4 primary/secondary goals with detailed success criteria.
