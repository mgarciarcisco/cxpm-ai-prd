## Section Guidelines

Provide supporting information:

**Include:**
- **Glossary**: Define technical terms, acronyms, and domain-specific language used in the PRD
- **Assumptions**: List assumptions made during PRD creation that should be validated
- **Out of Scope**: Explicitly list what is NOT included in this initiative (to prevent scope creep)
- **Related Documents**: References to related PRDs, design docs, research, or external resources
- **Version History**: Note this is version 1.0 of the PRD
- **Stakeholders**: Key stakeholders and their roles (if identifiable from requirements)

**Format:**

### Glossary
| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| ... | ... |

### Assumptions
1. Assumption 1 - [Needs validation by: stakeholder/date]
2. Assumption 2 - [Needs validation by: stakeholder/date]

### Out of Scope
- Item 1 (reason)
- Item 2 (reason)

### Related Documents
- [Document Name](link) - Description

Length: Comprehensive but concise supporting information.
