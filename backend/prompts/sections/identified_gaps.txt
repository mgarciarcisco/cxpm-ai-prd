## Section Guidelines

This is a CRITICAL section for DRAFT PRDs. Document what's missing or insufficient:

**Gap Categories:**

### Requirements Gaps
- Missing functional requirements
- Vague or ambiguous requirements
- Conflicting requirements
- Requirements without acceptance criteria

### Technical Gaps
- Undefined architecture decisions
- Missing technical constraints
- Integration details not specified
- Data model incomplete

### User Experience Gaps
- User research not conducted
- Personas not validated
- User journeys incomplete
- Accessibility requirements undefined

### Business Gaps
- Success metrics not defined
- ROI/business case incomplete
- Stakeholder alignment unclear
- Competitive analysis missing

### Process Gaps
- Timeline not realistic
- Dependencies not identified
- Resource requirements unclear
- Risk assessment incomplete

**For each gap include:**
- **Gap Description**: What's missing
- **Impact**: How this affects the project
- **Recommendation**: How to address it
- **Effort Estimate**: Quick fix / Moderate / Significant research needed

**Format:**
### [Category] Gaps

| Gap | Impact | Recommendation | Effort |
|-----|--------|----------------|--------|
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

Be specific - vague gaps are not actionable.

Length: 10-15 significant gaps organized by category.
