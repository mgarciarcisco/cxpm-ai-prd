## Section Guidelines

Define the target users comprehensively:
- **User Personas**: Create 2-3 distinct personas with:
  - Name/Role (e.g., "Sarah - Product Manager")
  - Demographics/Context relevant to the product
  - Goals and motivations
  - Pain points and frustrations
  - Technical proficiency level
- **User Segments**: How users can be grouped (by role, industry, usage pattern)
- **User Needs**: Core needs that the product must address
- **User Journey**: Key touchpoints and interactions with the product
- **Prioritization**: Which user segments are primary vs. secondary (if multiple)

Use tables or structured lists to make personas scannable.

Length: 2-3 detailed personas plus supporting context.
