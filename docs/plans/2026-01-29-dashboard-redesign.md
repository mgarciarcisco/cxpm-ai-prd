## Key Changes from Current Design

1. **Removed "New Project" as primary action** - Projects are created by saving Quick Convert results or from the projects section
2. **Quick Convert capabilities are front and center** - No extra click to access them
3. **INPUT/OUTPUT clarity** - Each card explains what goes in and what comes out
4. **"START A TASK" header** - Action-oriented language
5. **Visual hierarchy** - Tasks first, then projects

## Design Decisions

1. **"New Project" removed** - Projects are created only via "Save to Project" flow after generating content

2. **Card height** - All cards same height for visual consistency

3. **Coming Soon cards** - Generate PRD, Recommend Features, and Mockups are marked as "Coming Soon"

4. **Active cards** - Convert Meeting Notes to Requirements and User Stories link to their Quick Convert pages
